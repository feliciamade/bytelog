Glossary

Artifical Intelligence

+

Umbrella term for a variety of technologies and approaches that use algorithms to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Machine Learning

+

A subfield of AI that uses statistical algorithums to teach computers how to learn from data and perform tasks without being explicity programmed.

Neural Networks

+

A type of machine learning model inspired by the humanbrain, where interconnected nodes (neurons) process information in layers to produce outputs.
